## 1. Who we are
[APP NAME] is a personal fitness, nutrition and wellbeing app.
Contact: **[YOUR CONTACT EMAIL]**.

## 2. The information we handle

**a) Information you create in the app.** This includes the things you enter or
generate while using it, such as: workouts completed, steps, water, sleep hours,
mood check-ins and notes, calorie/food logs, fasting sessions, skin-care answers,
your feeding budget, and your name and goals if you choose to add them.

**b) Account information (only if you log in).** If you create an account, we
collect your **email address** and a securely managed password, handled for us by
Google Firebase Authentication. We never see or store your password ourselves.

**c) Information we do NOT collect.** We do not collect your precise location, your
contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers. The app contains **no ads** and
**no third-party trackers**.

## 3. Where your information is stored
- If you **do not log in**, your information stays **on your device only**. We
  cannot see it.
- If you **log in**, your information is also stored in our secure cloud database
  (**Google Firebase / Firestore**) so it can sync across your devices. Access is
  restricted so that only your account can read or write your data.

## 4. How we use your information
We use it only to:
- run the app's features and show you your own progress;
- save and sync your data across your devices when you're logged in.

We do **not** sell your information, and we do **not** use it for advertising.

## 5. Sharing
We do not share your personal information with anyone, except:
- **Service providers** who operate the app's infrastructure on our behalf —
  currently **Google Firebase** (authentication and database). Their handling of
  data is governed by Google's privacy terms.
- **If required by law**, or to protect rights and safety.

The app may show links (e.g. "Watch demo") that open **YouTube** in your browser.
Once you leave the app, the other website's own privacy policy applies.

## 6. Health & wellbeing data
Some of what you log (mood, sleep, weight-related habits) is personal wellbeing
information. We treat it with extra care, store it only as described above, and
never use it for any purpose other than providing the app to you. The app is for
general wellbeing and is **not a medical device** and not a substitute for
professional medical advice.

## 7. Your choices and rights
- **See or edit** your data anytime inside the app.
- **Export** your data using the in-app backup code.
- **Delete** your data: use "Reset all my data" in the app to clear your device,
  and contact us at **[YOUR CONTACT EMAIL]** to delete your cloud account and its
  data. We will action deletion requests within a reasonable time.
- Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the **Nigeria Data
  Protection Act / NDPR**, and — for users in the EU/UK — the **GDPR**, including
  rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your data.

## 8. Data retention
We keep your cloud data for as long as your account exists. When you ask us to
delete your account, we remove your stored data.

## 9. Children
The app is intended for users **[CHOOSE: 13 / 16 / 18]** and older. We do not
knowingly collect data from children below that age. If you believe a child has
created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

## 10. Security
We use Google Firebase's industry-standard security, encryption in transit, and
access rules that restrict each account to its own data. No system is 100% secure,
but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.
