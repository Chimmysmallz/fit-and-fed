# Play Store listing copy — Fit & Fed

limits are noted; everything here fits.

## App title  (limit: 30 characters)
```
Fit & Fed: Workouts & Meals
```

## Short description  (limit: 80 characters)
```
Workouts, mood & sleep tracking, and budget-friendly meal plans in one app.
```

## Full description  (limit: 4000 characters)
```
Struggling to stay consistent? Fit & Fed makes it easy to move more, rest better, and eat well — all in one simple, motivating app that works even when you're offline.

Whether you have 5 minutes or 30, Fit & Fed meets you where you are. No gym, no equipment, no pressure — just small wins that add up.

🏋️ WORKOUTS THAT FIT YOUR DAY
• 9 guided routines, from a 5-minute “Wake Up & Go” to full-body, core, legs, upper body and cardio
• A 27-move exercise library with animated demos so you always know what to do
• Built-in timers, rest periods and on-screen cues — just press start and follow along

🧘 WELLNESS, ALL IN ONE PLACE
• Mood check-ins with weekly insights
• Sleep log and bedtime reminders
• Hydration and step tracking
• A skin-care helper with a simple daily routine
• Desk-friendly office stretches and break reminders to fight the “sitting all day” slump

🍽️ EAT WELL WITHOUT OVERSPENDING
• Tell the app your weekly feeding budget and get an affordable shopping list and easy meal ideas
• A calorie counter built around real, everyday foods
• An intermittent fasting timer with simple plans (16:8, 18:6, 20:4)

🎮 STAY MOTIVATED
• Earn XP, level up, and collect coins and badges
• Take on a fresh daily challenge
• Unlock colourful themes as you progress
• Keep your streak alive — consistency beats intensity

☁️ YOUR PROGRESS, YOUR WAY
• Works fully offline — your data stays private on your phone
• Optional sign-in to sync your progress across devices
• No ads. No trackers. No clutter.

Fit & Fed is for real people with busy lives who want to build healthier habits one day at a time. Start small, stay consistent, and feel the difference.

Note: Fit & Fed supports general wellbeing and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Food prices and calorie values are estimates and may vary.

Your health, your pace. Let’s go. 💪
```
