This Privacy Policy explains how Fit & Fed (“the app”, “we”, “us”), provided by [YOUR NAME OR BUSINESS NAME], handles your information. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Fit & Fed is a personal fitness, nutrition and wellbeing app. Contact: [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL].

2. The information we handle

a) Information you create in the app. The things you enter or generate while using it — workouts completed, steps, water, sleep hours, mood check-ins and notes, calorie/food logs, fasting sessions, skin-care answers, your feeding budget, and your name and goals if you choose to add them.

b) Account information (only if you log in). If you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely managed password, handled for us by Google Firebase Authentication. We never see or store your password ourselves.

c) What we do NOT collect. We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers. The app contains no ads and no third-party trackers.

3. Where your information is stored

If you do not log in, your information stays on your device only — we cannot see it. If you log in, your information is also stored in our secure cloud database (Google Firebase / Firestore) so it can sync across your devices. Access is restricted so only your account can read or write your data.

4. How we use your information

Only to run the app's features, show you your own progress, and sync your data across your devices when you're logged in. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

5. Sharing

We do not share your personal information, except: with service providers who run the app's infrastructure on our behalf (currently Google Firebase — authentication and database), or if required by law or to protect rights and safety. Links such as “Watch demo” open YouTube in your browser; once you leave the app, that site's own privacy policy applies.

6. Health & wellbeing data

Some of what you log (mood, sleep, weight-related habits) is personal wellbeing information. We treat it with extra care, store it only as described above, and never use it for any other purpose. The app is for general wellbeing and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional medical advice.

7. Your choices and rights

You can see or edit your data anytime in the app, export it with the in-app backup code, and delete it (use “Reset all my data” to clear your device, and contact us at [YOUR CONTACT EMAIL] to delete your cloud account and its data). Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Act / NDPR, and — for users in the EU/UK — the GDPR, including rights to access, correct, delete or restrict use of your data.

8. Data retention

We keep your cloud data while your account exists. When you ask us to delete your account, we remove your stored data.

9. Children

The app is intended for users [CHOOSE: 13 / 16 / 18] and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age; if you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use Google Firebase's industry-standard security, encryption in transit, and access rules that restrict each account to its own data. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We'll change the “Last updated” date above, and note significant changes in the app or by email where appropriate.
